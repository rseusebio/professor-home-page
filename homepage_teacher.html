Short Bio

Charles Xavier is an associate professor at the Computer Science department - DCC - at Federal University of Rio de Janeiro - UFRJ. He has a degree in Computer Engineering from Harvard (2004); obtained her MSc (2007) and PhD degrees (2012) in Computer Science from the ESTÁCIO. He was a substitute professor at the Department of Applied Informatics of UFRJ and a post-doc researcher at the Department of Informatics of the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro - PUC-Rio. His main research interests are Robots, AI, Machine Learning and Deep Learning.
